Welcome To Delta Prestige.

Delta Prestige, a landmark residential project by Balaji Group, redefines luxury living in Panvel with its premium Ground + 14 storeyed tower, offering spacious 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and 5 BHK combinations. Strategically located near Khandeshwar Station, it provides seamless connectivity to Sion-Panvel Highway, JNPT, upcoming metro lines, and Navi Mumbai-Pune Hyperloop. Built on a 6000 sq. mt. CIDCO tender plot, the project boasts ultra-premium amenities such as valet parking, smart access cards, a car spa, EV charging, concierge services, and 3-tier security with smart door locks and cameras. With proximity to top schools, hospitals, shopping malls, and business hubs, Delta Prestige ensures a perfect blend of luxury, convenience, and future-ready infrastructure.The project features a grand double-height lobby, five lifts per floor, and spacious layouts designed for ultimate comfort. With its elite amenities and prime location, Delta Prestige sets a new benchmark for luxury living in Panvel.
