Welcome To Delta Prestige.

Delta Prestige by Balaji Group offers luxury living in Panvel with a Ground + 14 tower featuring spacious 2, 3, and 5 BHKs. Located near Khandeshwar Station, it ensures excellent connectivity to highways, metro lines, and the Navi Mumbai-Pune Hyperloop. Built on a 6000 sq. mt. CIDCO plot, it boasts valet parking, smart access, a car spa, EV charging, concierge services, and 3-tier security. With top schools, hospitals, malls, and business hubs nearby, Delta Prestige blends luxury, convenience, and future-ready infrastructure.
